Introducing...

Hello! My name is Chantrice, but I prefer to go by Ace. I am 28 years old, a single mom, and I have dreams of being an artist. I love creating new things, seeing my ideas come to life, and open up dialogue about things that I feel should be talked about more within the community. I enjoy photography, dance, graphic design, photomanipulations, as well as creating sets or props for physical shoots or film. I am currently working on getting into the film industry as a scenic painter and becoming unionized within that craft before potentially moving to costumes. Aside from that goal, I also want to become a licensed Art Therapist for children under the age of 12. My last creative side quest is to be the go to creative director for brand building. From logos, to websites and applications, to merch, product photography, etc. I have my interests in a lot of different pots, but I am confident in my ability to make it all happen even better than the way I imagine it in my head.
